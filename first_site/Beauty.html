HERE ARE 3 DIY MASKS YOU CAN MAKE AT HOME! Avocado Hair Mask What it does: Avocado is rich in Vitamin E and it helps to repair damaged hair cuticles. Banana is loaded with Vitamin B and Potassium which helps with hydration. Maple Syrup has humectant properties that lock in moisture. Ingredients: 1/2 Small Avocado 1/2 Banana 2 Tablespoons Maple Syrup  Instructions: Puree all ingredients and apply to hair. Leave on 15 minutes. Lightly shampoo, followed by conditioner *  *  * Papaya Face Mask What it does: The enzymes in papayas will help you achieve smooth and supple skin while the lactic acid in yogurt dissolves dead skin cells and soothes sun burns.  Ingredients: 1/2 Cup Mashed Papaya 1/8 Cup Yogurt 1 Teaspoon Lemon Juice  Instructions: Puree all ingredients and apply to cleansed face. Leave on 10 minutes maximum. Rinse with cool water and apply a light moisturizer.  *  *  *  Strawberry Hair Mask What it does: The enzymes in strawberries help retain moisture and the coconut and jojoba oils are the perfect moisturizer for dry, parched hair.  Ingredients: 2 Tablespoons Coconut Oil 1 Tablespoon Jojoba Oil 6 Strawberries Instructions: Blend ingredients together. Apply the mixture to hair and leave on 15 minutes. Then rinse out with warm water.
